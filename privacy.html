Privacy Pocicy

Last Edited on 2016-11-23

User accounts and collection of information

You are not required to create a user account or provide any personal information in order to use this service without sync feature. However, you are required to create a user account if you use sync feature. During registration, you are required to provide password and valid email address.

Collection of information as part of using this service

In addition to the information that you provide to us when you register for a user account, we collect information about stickies: the URLs, titles, tags, content of the stickies and other sticky information (color, size, position etc.).

Use of information

We use the information we collect to operate our services, such as saving stickies to backend server, you use to sync stickies through the backend server. However we will not use these stickies inforamation and email address for any other purpose.

Security

Your personal information is contained behind secured networks and is only accessible by a limited number of persons who have special access rights to such systems, and are required to keep the information confidential. In addition, all information you supply is encrypted via Secure Socket Layer (SSL) technology. We implement a variety of security measures when a user enters, submits, or accesses their information to maintain the safety of your personal information.
